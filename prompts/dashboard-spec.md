# MoneyMind Dashboard Specification v1.0

---

## PURPOSE

The dashboard is the core of the product.

It must:
- interpret data  
- guide decisions  
- reduce friction  

---

## STRUCTURE

### 1. HERO

Short message:
- 1 sentence  
- directional  

Example:
"Your structure is clearer. What you do next matters."

---

### 2. AI INSIGHT (CORE BLOCK)

Structure:

- What You See  
- Why It Matters  
- Think About  
- Next Step  

Rules:
- max 4–5 lines  
- no long text  

---

### 3. RECOMMENDED NEXT STEP

- only ONE action  
- clear CTA  

Example:
"Map your capital structure"

---

### 4. GUIDED FREEDOM

2–3 optional actions:

- Understand your capital  
- Check your leakage  
- Improve your strategy  

---

### 5. ACADEMY LINK

Light integration:

"Learn why this matters → [Lesson]"

---

### 6. PROGRESS

Show:

- tool completion  
- last updated  
- flow status  

---

## UX RULES

- minimal input  
- no clutter  
- action first  
- clarity over detail  

---

## SUCCESS

User should feel:
"I know what to do next"
