# MoneyMind Academy Blueprint

The MoneyMind Academy is the educational layer of the MoneyMind platform.

Its purpose is to improve financial clarity, financial understanding and long-term wealth decision-making.

MoneyMind does not aim to teach people how to speculate or chase short-term gains.  
Instead, it focuses on helping individuals understand how money works, how wealth is built, and how financial behavior affects long-term outcomes.

The academy complements the MoneyMind tools and follows the core platform philosophy:

Tool → Insight → Lesson → Better Decision

---

# Learning Model

The academy follows a **guided learning path** while also allowing users to explore topics freely.

Two learning modes exist:

**Guided Path**
- Linear progression through the academy
- Lessons count toward progress
- Contributes to Financial Insight Level

**Explore Mode**
- Users can browse any lesson
- No progress logging
- Designed for curiosity and reference

---

# Lesson Format

MoneyMind lessons follow **Model A**.

Duration:
5–7 minutes reading time  
Approximately 600–900 words.

Lesson structure:

1. Concept  
2. Why it matters  
3. Real-world example  
4. Key insight  
5. Reflection question

The goal is clarity and practical understanding rather than academic depth.

---

# Learning Phases

The academy is divided into four learning phases.

## Phase 1 — Financial Foundations
Understanding how money and financial systems work.

## Phase 2 — Building Wealth
Understanding how capital grows and how different assets behave.

## Phase 3 — Wealth Architecture
Understanding how wealth is structured and allocated.

## Phase 4 — Wealth Protection
Understanding how wealth is preserved over time.
