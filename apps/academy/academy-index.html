Tool Guides

What your numbers mean

Short explanations of what each MoneyMind tool measures and why it matters.

Capital Map

Maps your financial structure into four buckets: liquid (accessible cash), invested (deployed capital), locked (illiquid assets), and debt. The goal is to see your real net worth — not just your income.

Spending vs Building

Splits your income into what gets consumed versus what builds your future. Your building ratio — the % that goes toward wealth — is the single most important number in your financial life.

Wealth Leakage

Identifies spending that adds no real value. Subscriptions, impulse buys, convenience spend. Not all spending is leakage — but some of it quietly destroys your future without you noticing.

The Builder

Takes your Capital Map, Spending, and Leakage data and generates a personal action plan. Priorities are determined by rules; the plan itself is written by AI based on your exact numbers.
